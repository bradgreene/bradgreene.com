Brad Greene writes and illustrates books, comics, and graphic novels. He is currently drawing a comic titled BLACK EYE, which follows a character through a dark fantasy world of dreamlike landscapes and a labyrinth of tunnels beneath the mountain city of Tokyotown.

He earned his BFA in illustration at Kendall College of Art and Design in 2010.

He also records ambient music that inhabits the same world as the comics, bringing another layer of atmosphere to the story. Songs are released for free under the name SKELETON (click the skull on this page to listen).
